description: Learn how dropins define all of our Commerce storefronts.

Dropins are storefront components, like Product Details, Cart, and Checkout, that can be
dropped-into existing sites to quickly create fast and adaptable storefronts. We built our dropins
so that designers and writers can work alongside developers to get your storefronts into
production as soon as possible.
