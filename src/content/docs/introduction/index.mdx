### Edge Delivery Services

The set of web development methods and tools that maximize site performance and visibility. They include [performance-first architecture](https://business.adobe.com/products/experience-manager/sites/site-performance.html), [flexible authoring](https://business.adobe.com/products/experience-manager/sites/content-creation.html), [website experimentation](https://business.adobe.com/products/experience-manager/sites/testing-optimization.html), [realtime analytics](https://business.adobe.com/products/experience-manager/sites/testing-optimization.html#:~:text=50%2D25%2D25.-,Content,-insights), and [fast development workflows](https://business.adobe.com/products/experience-manager/sites/developer-tools.html).

### Commerce dropins

Full-featured shopping components that turn websites into storefronts. Dropins are not primitive components, like Carousels and Galleries. They provide the entire storefront shopping experience for a website.
