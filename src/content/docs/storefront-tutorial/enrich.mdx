To attract more shoppers, you may want to "enrich" the product attributes and descriptions from your Commerce data by adding promotional content from another source. As you learned in Extend with slots, you can add content directly to a dropin using slots. But this doesn't scale when you need to add content to entire categories within your catalog. To solve this, we use a technique called "folder mapping". Let's learn about it here.
